Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cosmos Holo analyze card centering?
Cosmos Holo uses advanced computer vision algorithms to detect card edges and calculate precise centering measurements. All analysis happens locally on your device for privacy.
Is my card data stored in the cloud?
No, all your card images and analysis data are stored locally on your device. We never upload your cards to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
How accurate are the centering measurements?
Our algorithm provides sub-millimeter accuracy for centering analysis. Results may vary based on image quality and lighting conditions.
Can I export my analysis results?
Yes, you can save analysis results with custom names and export them for your records or grading submissions.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime through your device's App Store or Google Play Store subscription settings. Your access continues until the current billing period ends.
